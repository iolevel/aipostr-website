F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Our focus is on the automation of repetitive, informative content using various workflow triggers. The goal is to remove the burden of having to think about producing content by automating the generation of social media posts as much as possible.

We wouldn't recommend relying only on our service for your social media. The tool works perfectly for informative or repetitive content, but you should mix in human-generated posts as well in order to engage your target audience.

We offer a number of campaign templates that are tried and tested to work. However, you can program your own triggers using Node.js; check out this article in our docs, which shows you how to add your own triggers.

Keep in mind that AIPOSTR uses artificial intelligence to produce your content. You can influence the style of your posts by adding more specific instructions to the prompt. However, AIPOSTR does not aim to fully replace humans and you should mix in some original content occasionally.

We are working hard to add new features all the time and we welcome any feedback from our users. Please don't hesitate to contact us with any improvement suggestions.

At the moment, we don't have a discount for students; however, every new account gets $5 in free credits to try AIPOSTR.
